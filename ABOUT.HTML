How to Use the Platform

Follow these simple steps to make the most of our platform:

  1. Customer clicks the "Buy" button: Initiates the action and redirects to the product page.
  2. Add product to the product list: Select and specify product details.
  3. Product list interaction options: Edit, add more, or delete products.
  4. Finalize the product list: Ready the list for checkout.
  5. Cart review: Review and make final changes to the items.
  6. Proceed to payment: Choose payment options like credit card or PayPal.
  7. Payment confirmation: Receive an order confirmation upon successful payment.
